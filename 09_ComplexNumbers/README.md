# Module 09: Complex Numbers

This module provides a comprehensive suite of algorithms for complex number analysis, extending beyond basic arithmetic to include polar forms, roots of unity, and complex functions.

## Chapters

1.  **Basics**: Representation and fundamental properties.
2.  **Operations**: Advanced algebraic operations.
3.  **Polar Form**: Modulus-argument form and conversions.
4.  **Euler's Formula**: Exponential representation.
5.  **Roots**: Finding nth roots of unity and complex numbers.
6.  **Functions**: Complex exponential, logarithmic, and trigonometric functions.
7.  **Mappings**: Conformal mappings and transformations.
8.  **Applications**: Signal processing basics and fractals.
