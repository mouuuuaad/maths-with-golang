# Module 01: Calculus

This module covers the fundamental concepts of Calculus, integrating limits, derivatives, and integrals to solve mathematical problems.

## Chapters

1.  **Introduction**: Overview of functions and graphs.
2.  **Limits Fundamentals**: Basic limit laws and continuity.
3.  **Continuity**: Intermediate Value Theorem and properties.
4.  **Differentiation**: Tangent lines and rates of change.
5.  **Integration**: Riemann sums and the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
6.  **Series**: Infinite series and convergence tests.
7.  **Multivariable**: Vectors and partial derivatives basics.
8.  **Applications**: Area between curves, volumes of revolution, and work.
